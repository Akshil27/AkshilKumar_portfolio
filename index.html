My Philosophy & Approach

Data analysis, for me, is as much about people as it is about numbers. Early in my career I learned that a successful dashboard isn’t just a set of charts—it’s a narrative that resonates with the audience. My philosophy is to begin every project by understanding the stakeholder’s story: What decision are they trying to make? What pain points are they experiencing? Only then can I craft solutions that truly matter. I strive to balance technical rigour with empathy, ensuring that insights are both accurate and actionable.

Throughout my journey I’ve discovered the power of ownership and continuous improvement. I like to think of projects as partnerships: I take responsibility for shepherding ideas from exploratory analysis through implementation, keeping open lines of communication along the way. I constantly challenge myself to simplify complex processes, automate repetitive tasks and explore new techniques. Curiosity drives me forward—whether that’s learning a new visualisation tool or diving into an unfamiliar dataset. This mindset helps me adapt quickly and deliver solutions that evolve with changing business needs.
