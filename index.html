Leadership Philosophy

Instead of merely repeating Amazon’s Leadership Principles, I’ve woven them into my own professional philosophy. These guiding themes shape how I approach analytics projects, collaborate with teammates and drive impact across the supply chain. Here’s how I bring them to life in my work:

  • Customer Obsession: I always start with the needs of internal and external customers when designing data solutions. My dashboards and models focus on answering the right questions so that end users are delighted and empowered.
  • Ownership: I take full responsibility for projects from data modelling through implementation and follow‑up. When challenges arise, I lean in, communicate transparently and proactively seek improvements rather than waiting for direction.
  • Invent & Simplify: I look for creative ways to simplify complex processes. Whether designing an automated report or a forecasting algorithm, I challenge assumptions and build intuitive tools that make decision‑making easy for stakeholders.
  • Learn & Be Curious: I continuously expand my skill set—be it exploring new BI platforms, studying emerging supply chain trends or experimenting with predictive models. This curiosity helps me stay ahead of the curve and bring fresh perspectives to each problem.
